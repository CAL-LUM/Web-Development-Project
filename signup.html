Registering Information


By creating an account with 'Title of website' you are able to view stolen bikes in your area, reigster multiple bikes for you and your family, and report bikes stolen.


To create an account, complete the form on this page with your details.



Registering an account is completely free and provides you with the following benefits:



  • A lifetime registration on the 'title of website' database.

  • A registration logbook, you can use this as proof of ownership in case of theft, should you chose to register a bike.

  • Secure access online to manage your account and registered bikes.



Your account details are held within our database, which your local police force has access to in order to view stolen bikes, as well as contact the owners of these bikes.
