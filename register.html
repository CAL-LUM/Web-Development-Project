Registering Information

Registering your bike is a crucial step in ensuring its safety and increasing the chances of recovery in case of theft. By registering your bike, a digital record is created, which can be invaluable in proving ownership, should your bike be stolen or lost.


Register your bikes details in the form on this page.



Registering your bike is completely free and provides you with the following benefits:



  • A lifetime registration on the 'title of website' database.

  • Your bike will appear in 'my bikes', you can use this to report your bike stolen and as proof of ownership in case of theft.

  • Secure access online to manage your account and registered bikes.



Your account details are held within our database, which your local police force has access to in order to view stolen bikes, as well as contact the owners of these bikes.
